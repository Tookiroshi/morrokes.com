Cyo Rules

For starters, here are the general rules before creating a CYO:

  • - 1 Character CYO per person. (since there is a re-opening, we all start fresh, if you participated in the 2019 MYO event you can make a CYO)
  • -CYO's are NOT account bound, this means when you create your character, you can trade,sale... as any other character. Be careful though! Once you've created a cyo and you trade it, you are not able to crate a second one.
  • -Cheating, creating a second CYO for any reason, either under your main accout or secondary account will result in a blacklist.
  • -You can either create a Silgan or a Morroke.No companions allowed.
  • -You are not able to paint more than one solid color on the CYO creation. Pupils should be black and the morroke tank liquid should be the same color as the color choosen. This means no multiple colors,patterns,mutations.. on the creation aplication. You will br able to customize your character after the approval of the CYO. with Mutation fruits and Dye fruits .
