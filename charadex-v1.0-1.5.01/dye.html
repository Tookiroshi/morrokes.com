Dye Fruit

The dry fruit is a fruit that can be used to change the color of your character, found in almost any market around Vahonea, this fruit is popular amongst fashon trenders. Sweet and yummy and full of crunchy tinny seeds, learn more here.

How to use the dye Fruit

The dye fruit is quite easy to use, consume it, and change your colors as you desire!

Ready to change yourself or your companion? Do so here!

NOTE:Nails,hair,beard,eyes,makeup,frekles, are free to change whenever, no fruit needed
