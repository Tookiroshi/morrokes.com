Tanks are horns situated on the top or sides of the head of a Morrke, made from very durable sort of hollow glass, they can be any sort of shape and size.

The inside holds a sort of thick liquid, goop, created from consuming charcoal and digesting it creating a transparent or opaque liquid,It is used as energy storage.
