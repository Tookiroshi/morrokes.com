Morrokes

Small but fierce, this curious adventurous creatures are one of the two sentient cratures of the world of Vahonea! Around 140cm tall,this cretures have a great sense of community.

Decendants of the god of creation, Morrokes thrive from diversity, their most visual factor? Their horns. But...

What are the horns all about?

Tanks are horns situated on the top or sides of the head of a Morrke, made from very durable sort of hollow glass, they can be any sort of shape and size. The inside holds a sort of thick liquid, goop, created from consuming charcoal and digesting it creating a transparent or opaque liquid,It is used as energy storage.

Though they are omnivorious creatures, most of their stamina/energy comes from the charcoal they consume. Like so, they create a sort of thick mist, created as a defense mechanism, or, to create portals!(TBA)
