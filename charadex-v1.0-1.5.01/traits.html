Mutation Fruit

The mutation fruit is a very secial type of fruit native to Vahonea. Its said that whoever, or whatever consumes said fruit, is its given the trait they desire the most. Its only eaten in special ocasions by most. Some consume it for enhancements, some for looks only, whatever the case may be, some belive its a gift from the gods. You can find them in select markets in Vahonea. Since now a days, some vendors at the market have found the fruit is quite profitable. Others find it a blasphemy to play with your own DNA.
