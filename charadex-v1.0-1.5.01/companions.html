Companions

Companions are wild fauna that have been domesticated by Morrokes and Silgans to help with tasks, or simply to have some company.

Garbles

This strong and sturdy companion is nothing but short of power, docile, but not dumb, its glass stretchy neck can be used as a way of transporting items. Its powerful body, can be used as a mount, or, its also gerat to tow farm lands.

They eat through their mouth,and it will end in their stomach as any other being through a hole at the end of their glass neck. Though, with one set of teeth, they only use it to cut grass or twigs, as they are herbivores.

Pallas

Pallas might be smaller than garbles and they don't have great stamina regardless, but their aerodynamic body makes them travel distaces fast, in short periods of time. But that is not all, their glass faces contain a gas that is capable of creating short distance portals. They use it to go form one side of a cliff to another, or go to another place fast. Since they don't have eyes, their touch and other senses are hightened.

Ankoras

This thin but fast thinking creatures are all speed. Their size can vary a lot, from very small to mount size, depending on their diet. Their "wings" are just for gliding short distances. They actually have arms tucked inisde their wings, its a sort of sleve they put on to "have" wings.

Oobs

This small but stretchy companions are your perfect way to move across difficult land! They are round and fuffy but their surprisingly strong grip supports a lot of weight to use it as a zipline or rope to fling your way with ease.

Bonsai

This twiggy companion is the definition of alive transportation, made from the same milenary trees as O'kres this come-alive twigs, depending on how many items it carries inside its self made silky cloack, it magically becomes smaller or bigger. The bigger the slower it becomes. It sees and feeds through the the soil, through its holes on hands and feet. So, you will rarley see them roaming in cities.

Its said it has a strong culture around masks,it always wears a sort of mask on its face. The heavier the mask the stronger the Bonsai it says it is. The lighter the mask, the more pretty and deicate the Bonsai it says it is.

Fauna

The fauna is the life the world of Vahonea offers, creatures all living in a wild cohesive ecosistem. This creatures are not tameable.

Eebos

The terror of the sky, this agressive solitary big or tinny creatures are the predecessors of obees. Their tendrill like antenna work as a solar powered way to fly, so you will rareley see them fly at night or in rainy arears. They only kill to eat, mostly big meaty animals, but i woudn't approach one too much.

Lagos

They are seal-like creatures that with their big sharp teeth they scrape and open clamis to eat them. They are quite lazy creatures, they dont tend to move a lot to maintain energy since they live in colder areas.

Isirs (wip name)

This monkey-like creaure lives in the depth of the rainforest areas of Vahonea. On the top of bug infested trees, you will find this little creatures. I wount aproach them too much, have food on hand or be warned that they do tend to bite tourists and locals that go visit their areas.

O'kres

This very special creature comes alive from the millenary silk trees of Vahonea, when the tree dies or is chopped down at old age, the tree emmerges form the main log a sort of quadruped beast. Tamed in older ages by Silgans, now, wild glorious beasts roam the old forests protecting the milenary trees.

Their are full of knowldege but i would be cautious around them, they don't like company.
