First of all... What is an ARPG?

An ARPG (Art RPG) is a subgenre of role-playing games (RPGs) that emphasizes artistic expression, visual storytelling, and creative world-building. Unlike traditional RPGs, which often focus on combat, stats, and mechanics, ARPGs prioritize aesthetics, narrative, and emotional engagement. These games often feature hand-drawn or highly stylized visuals, unique art styles, and immersive environments that feel like interactive art pieces!

ARPGs were developed on DeviantART, likely around the time when groups became a site feature.(Around 2009)

Masterlist? Myo?

Thank you for reathing further and wanting to know more about us, and the comunity, and the world surrounding ARPG's!

A Masterlist, is a comprehensive and organized list or database that documents all officially approved characters, designs, MYOS and members of a CS or ARPG. It serves as a reference for both the creator(s) of the species and the community, ensuring transparency, authenticity, and proper tracking of the species' characters and their owners.

And Myo?

A Create Your Own (MYO) is, essentially, a special permission granted by a ticket, that allows individuals to design their own character within the species rules and guidelines. Unlike standard closed species/ARPG's designs, which are typically created and sold by the species owner(s) or guest artists. A MYO gives the holder the freedom to create a unique character while still adhering to the species design requirements.
